Customised Lead Generation Solutions Aligned with Your Marketing Goals

At ReyMarTech, we understand that each business has unique needs, so our lead generation solutions are tailored to capture and nurture the right prospects at every stage. From building brand awareness to identifying purchase-ready buyers, we offer targeted solutions to drive your growth.

Single Touch

Our Single Touch solution captures early-stage interest with a simple form-fill engagement on one content piece. This approach builds top-of-funnel leads while raising brand visibility and awareness.

Double Touch

With Double Touch, we engage leads with two consecutive content pieces, driving product education and extending reach within your target audience. This strategy strengthens interest and connection with your brand.

High Quality Leads (HQL)

Our HQL solution goes deeper with a form-fill engagement plus a qualifying question. This mid-funnel approach identifies in-market leads with genuine interest, helping refine your focus on high-value prospects.

BANT (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline)

Designed for mid-funnel leads, BANT uses form-fill engagement with up to four qualifying questions. This solution provides insights into buyer readiness, empowering you to prioritise leads primed for purchase.

02How do you ensure the quality of leads generated?

Our proprietary data and in-depth qualification methods, such as BANT and HQL, help us filter out leads that are not genuinely interested. Each lead goes through engagement touchpoints, allowing us to gauge real interest before it reaches your pipeline.

03What’s the difference between Single Touch, Double Touch, HQL, and BANT solutions?

Each solution targets different stages of the funnel. Single and Double Touch focus on early-stage engagement for brand awareness, while HQL and BANT identify mid-funnel leads that show intent or purchase readiness, providing you with highly qualified prospects.
