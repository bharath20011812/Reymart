Our waterfall marketing process is carefully structured to identify, engage, and nurture leads, ensuring they align with your business goals. Here’s how we guide each prospect through the journey:

1

Audience Segmentation

We begin by identifying and segmenting your audience based on key characteristics. This targeted approach boosts relevance and engagement.

2

Content Engagement

3

Customised Waterfall Solutions to Meet Your Goals

At Rey MarTech, we understand that businesses require unique engagement strategies to attract and retain high-value prospects. Our waterfall marketing solutions offer targeted tiers to capture, educate, and convert leads across the funnel.

Active Buyers
  • Leads actively looking for your solutions are prioritised early in the campaign.
  • These high-intent prospects are sent directly for immediate follow-up.
Active Learnersh
  • For prospects showing interest but not immediate intent, we nurture them with relevant content.
  • This keeps your brand in mind as they consider options.
Top-of-Funnel Awareness
  • These leads are aware of your brand but need more nurturing.
  • Additional engagement tactics help build familiarity and interest.
In-Market Identifiers
  • These prospects may not be fully aware of your brand, but they are actively exploring options, allowing you to reach them early and establish trust before competitors.
